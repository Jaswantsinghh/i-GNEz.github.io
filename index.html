Working Process

Working Process of Fest

Here’s your guide to the tech fest 2021 process. Go through all the steps to know the exact process of the fest.

1-6 May, 2021

Registration

Pick your favourite event(s) and register in that event by filling the form corresponding to that event. Its that easy :)

6-9 May, 2021

Participation

Participate online. The links for your registered events will be sent to you via email and whatsapp groups. Use those links and show your talent.

9 May, 2021

Result Declaration

The ultimate genius will be revealed by our judging panel on 9th May, 2021 and the results will be announced on the whatsapp groups and will be mailed to you.

Shortly after result declaration

Prize Distribution

The winners will be contacted by our team for their addresses and the winning goodies will be sent at their addresses. Enjoy your prizes :)
